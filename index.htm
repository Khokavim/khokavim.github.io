What Is
PayAfrik?

PayAfrik is primarily a decentralised payment solution that leverages cryptocurrency to provide secure, transparent and faster means of transaction between trusted parties.

By using the native PayAfrik token called AfrikCoin (AFK), we are focused on building an extensive, decentralised online banking protocol which would disrupt conventional banks while providing traditional services for retail and institutional users.

We are made up of a team that has expert understanding and vast experience in programming and software development, blockchain technology and cryptocurrency, and also finance and investment.

Learn More

Cryptocurrency Wallet

PayAfrik offers a cryptocurrency wallet with which users can accept cryptocurrencies for services rendered and pay cryptocurrencies for services received.
The PayAfrik wallet serves as the entry point into the PayAfrik ecosystem and as such forms a vital part of users’ activities within the system.

The PayAfrik wallet is borderless and can receive and send value anywhere and with PayAfrik’s network of banks across Africa, we can provide liquidity to exchange any assets on the PayAfrik network.

P2P and B2B
Lending of crypto assets

PayAfrik allows users and businesses to support each other by facilitating lending with competitive interest rates and low overhead fees.

Africoin (afk)
The PayAfrik native Token

AFK is the native PayAfrik token that users can use to exchange value in the PayAfrik ecosystem. The token can be used as collateral to secure loans and other credit in the PayAfrik ecosystem

CryptoCurrency Payment Processor

For merchants on PayAfrik, the cryptocurrency payment processor is how PayAfrik handles merchant payments while eliminating price risk between initiation of the payment by the customer and receipt of the payment by the merchant.

This feature in combination with AFK tokens allows merchants to reduce the fees paid to third party payment processors to a bare minimum thus boosting revenue
